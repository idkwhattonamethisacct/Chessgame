How to play

  1. White moves first. Click a piece to see legal moves. Click a target square to move.
  2. The game ends on checkmate. A banner will prompt you to reset.
  3. Pawns promote to a queen on the last rank in this build. Castling and en passant are not included.

Chess basics

  • Checkmate: King is in check and no legal move can remove it.
